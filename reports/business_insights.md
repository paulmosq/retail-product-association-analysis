# Business Insights Report
## Retail Product Association Analysis — Ecuador Supermarket Context

**Dataset**: 10,000 synthetic transactions based on Ecuadorian supermarket 
purchasing patterns (Supermaxi / Mi Comisariato context)  
**Period**: January 2024 – December 2024  
**Analysis**: Chi-squared test, Cramér's V, Odds Ratio — segmented by time slot

---

## Key Finding 1 — Dairy + Snacks peak in the Afternoon

**Statistical result**: Dairy products and eggs + Snacks and drinks show 
their strongest association during Afternoon (Cramér's V = 0.1357), 
significantly weaker in Evening (V = 0.0759).

**Business recommendation**:
- Schedule cross-promotions between dairy and snack products specifically 
  during afternoon hours (12:00–18:00)
- Example: combo discount valid only between 12:00–17:00
- Ensure snack shelves are fully stocked before the afternoon rush

---

## Key Finding 2 — Fruits & Vegetables + Meats peak in the Evening

**Statistical result**: Fruits and vegetables + Meats and sausages association 
is strongest in Evening (Cramér's V = 0.1512) compared to Morning (V = 0.1216).

**Business recommendation**:
- Evening shoppers are likely preparing dinner — place produce and meat 
  sections in adjacent aisles
- Run evening promotions (18:00–21:00) combining fresh produce with 
  meat products
- Prioritize restocking both categories before 17:00

---

## Key Finding 3 — Home Cleaning + Snacks vary by time slot

**Statistical result**: Home cleaning + Snacks and drinks association is 
stronger in Morning (V = 0.1271) than Afternoon (V = 0.0831).

**Business recommendation**:
- Morning shoppers tend to do full household runs — bundle cleaning 
  products with snack promotions in the morning
- Consider loyalty points for morning purchases combining both categories

---

## General Recommendation

All 15 category pairs tested showed statistically significant associations 
(p < 0.05). However, the commercial priority should focus on pairs with 
the highest temporal variation — these represent the greatest opportunity 
for time-targeted promotions.

**Priority pairs for promotion scheduling:**

| Pair | Best Time Slot | Cramér's V |
|---|---|---|
| Dairy + Snacks | Afternoon | 0.1357 |
| Fruits & Veg + Meats | Evening | 0.1512 |
| Home Cleaning + Snacks | Morning | 0.1271 |
